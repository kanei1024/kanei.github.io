Brief Bio

I am currently a second year Ph.D student at Peking University, My research interests includes multimedia retrieval and deep learning. My recent works focus on Person Re-IDentification (ReID). Morever, I am interesed in some fundamental fields in computer vision, e.g., image classification and semantic segmentation.
