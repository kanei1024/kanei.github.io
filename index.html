Brief Bio

I am currently a 3rd year Ph.D student at Peking University under supervision by Prof. Shiliang Zhang. My research interests includes multimedia retrieval and deep learning. My recent works focus on image retrieval, person re-identification, self-supervised or semi-supervised Learning, pose estimation and some related fields.
