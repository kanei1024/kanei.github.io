Brief Bio
I am currently a 3rd year Ph.D student at Peking University under supervision by Prof. Shiliang Zhang. My research interests includes multimedia retrieval and deep learning. My recent works focus on Person Re-IDentification (ReID). Morever, I am interesed in some fundamental fields in computer vision, e.g., image classification and semantic segmentation.
